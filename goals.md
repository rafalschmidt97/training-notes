## Profile

- Desk job (programmer, 8-10h/day)
- Wedding: July 10, 2027 (~14 months away)

## Primary Goal
Be in the best shape of his life for his wedding. "Fit" aesthetic — lean, athletic, good performance — not bulky.

## Training Structure (Current/Planned)

### Strength — Gym (core priority)
- 2x/week gym with friend
- Previously had trainer program (Push/Pull/FBW) but strength has dropped since then — needs to rebuild
- Friend is flexible, can be convinced to follow a structured FBW-style program
- Previous peak numbers (NOT current): Squat ~95kg, Deadlift ~130kg, Pull-ups 10 reps BW

### Calisthenics (home supplement)
- Pull-up bar at home for days when gym isn't possible
- Quick sessions: pull-ups, dips, accessories
- New to calisthenics — needs progression guidance
- Purpose: consistency insurance, not a replacement for gym

### Cycling (summer)
- 1x/week (maybe 2x), group coffee rides + some threshold work
- 50-70km per ride
- Social/fun component

### Running (optional, low priority)
- Skeptical about sticking with it ("running is boring")
- 5k runs only, as time-filler when traveling or short on time
- Explicitly optional

### Mobility/Stretching
- Hates it but acknowledges importance
- Needs a minimal effective routine for desk worker
- Focus: hip flexors, thoracic spine, shoulders

## Weekly Template (Target)
- Mon: Gym FBW A (with friend)
- Tue: Rest or calisthenics (quick home session)
- Wed: Cycling (group ride, 50-70km)
- Thu: Gym FBW B (with friend)
- Fri: Rest or calisthenics
- Sat: Optional run/ride
- Sun: Rest
- Mobility: 10-15 min on 2-3 days (can be post-workout or evening)

## Key Observations
- Very consistent with training (5 sessions last week!)

- Sleep inconsistent — needs attention
- Works long desk hours — mobility/posture is a real need
